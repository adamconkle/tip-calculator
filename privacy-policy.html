Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/29/25

This Privacy Policy explains how Mint Tipper ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Mint Tipper mobile application and website.

1. Overview

Mint Tipper is a simple tip calculation tool. We designed it to work with as little data as possible. The mobile app does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its core features.

2. Information Collected by the Mobile App

The Mint Tipper mobile app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data such as your name, email address, payment details, or location.

The bill amounts and tip percentages you enter are processed locally on your device for calculation purposes only and are not sent to our servers.

3. Information Collected by the Website

If you visit the Mint Tipper website, standard web server logs may automatically record basic technical information, such as:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Referring website or link

This type of information is typically used in aggregate form to understand how the website is used and to maintain and improve the service.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Mint Tipper may use basic analytics tools or cookies on the website to better understand usage patterns and improve the experience. These tools generally collect aggregated, non-personally identifying information such as page views, approximate location, and device type.

You can usually control or block cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the site function.

5. Third-Party Links

The app and website may contain links to third-party websites (for example, legal pages, support pages, or other resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

6. Children's Privacy

Mint Tipper is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information associated with the Mint Tipper website. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices

Since the mobile app does not collect personal data, there is no account or profile to delete. If you no longer wish to use Mint Tipper, you may uninstall the app from your device at any time and stop using the website.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app or website after changes are posted will constitute your acceptance of those changes.
